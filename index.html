About Us

A Small Family Studio with a Global Story

Nadtochiy Photography is our small family business, created and run by two people who have dedicated their lives to telling wedding stories with honesty, beauty and care.

We started our career in 2008 in Zaporizhzhia, Ukraine, quickly becoming one of the most sought-after teams in our city. In 2014 we moved to Montenegro, where we spent almost a decade photographing destination weddings for couples from the UK, Canada, the USA, Australia and many other countries — often travelling to Croatia, Italy and France.

In 2024 we made Toronto our new home. Today we photograph and film weddings across the GTA, all of Ontario and beyond, bringing our destination-wedding experience to every celebration — whether it is an intimate elopement in the city or a large celebration in Niagara or Muskoka.

Our style is documentary, editorial, emotional, fine art, timeless and cinematic. We stay unobtrusive and observant, letting the day unfold naturally while making sure every important moment, guest and detail is carefully documented. At the same time, we know how to guide you through portraits and group photos in a gentle, flattering way.

The result is one complete story of your wedding day — diverse, rich in emotion and atmosphere, with images you will want to print, frame and revisit for years.

FAQ

Questions We Hear Most Often

A short overview of how we work. For full details and custom quotes, send us a note and tell us about your day.
How many photos will we receive?
We do not put a hard limit on the number of photos. We edit everything we shoot, so for a full wedding day you can expect around 700–1,000 fully edited images, depending on the length of coverage and your timeline.
When will we receive our gallery?
Wedding galleries are usually delivered within 3–4 months. We carefully select and edit every image, making sure the final story feels complete, cohesive and beautifully finished.
Do you work outside Toronto?
Yes. We regularly photograph and film weddings across the GTA, in Niagara, Muskoka and throughout Ontario. For more distant locations we often arrive the day before to avoid any travel issues and to scout the venue in advance.
Do you travel for destination weddings?
Absolutely. Thanks to our years in Montenegro we have extensive destination experience in coastal and historic locations. We frequently travel for weddings in Europe and North America and adapt quickly to new venues and light.
How do you approach posing?
Most of the day we work in a documentary way, observing and capturing things as they naturally happen. During couple portraits and group photos we gently guide you into flattering light and composition while keeping the mood relaxed and authentic.
Do you deliver RAW files?
No. We deliver carefully edited high‑resolution JPEG images that reflect our style and the story of your day. The editing process is a core part of our work and we want you to enjoy a polished final result.
Do you shoot on film?
Yes, we can include 35mm and/or medium format film in your coverage. Film adds a soft, timeless texture and colour palette that many of our couples love.
How do we book you?
Send us an inquiry through the form on our website or contact us directly by email, WhatsApp or Telegram. We will reply with availability, schedule a call and send a proposal with a contract and payment details. A 30% retainer secures your date.
