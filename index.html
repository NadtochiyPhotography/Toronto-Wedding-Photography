About Us

A small family studio with a global story

Nadtochiy Photography is our small family business, created and run by two people who have dedicated their lives to photographing and filming weddings. We are not an agency or a rotating team — when you book us, you get us.

Our journey began in 2008 in Zaporizhzhia, Ukraine, where we quickly became one of the leading wedding photographers in the city, trusted with the most luxurious celebrations. A few years later, we moved to Montenegro and spent nearly a decade capturing destination weddings along the Adriatic coast — in Montenegro, Croatia, Italy, France and beyond — for couples from Canada, the US, the UK, Australia and many other countries.

Today we are based in Toronto, Ontario, bringing this destination experience to weddings across the GTA, Niagara, Muskoka, and all of Ontario. Every year we photograph and film over a hundred stories: weddings, engagements, family sessions and proposals. Together we have captured more than a thousand weddings — each one treated as its own complete, carefully crafted narrative.

Our approach is quiet and observant. We stay in the background, never interrupting your day, but we are always present, noticing everything: the way your parents hold hands, the tears during the vows, the laughter at dinner, the way your friends look at you on the dance floor. We move a lot during the ceremony and reception, changing angles, capturing guests, wide scenes and small details — so that your final gallery feels rich and alive, not like a set of repeated frames.

Our Approach

How we tell the story of your day

We do not think of weddings as a set of individual photographs. For us, each wedding is one long, cohesive story — with its own rhythm, atmosphere, people and emotions.

Story-driven, not pose-driven

Our style can be described as documentary, editorial, emotional, fine art, timeless and cinematic — and all of these words are true. We try not to disturb the natural flow of your wedding day. Most of the time we are quietly observing: noticing the hugs, glances, laughter and tears that make your celebration unique.

During the ceremony we do not stay in one place. We move, change angles, photograph you and your guests, wide scenes and close-up details. Because of this, your gallery is rich and varied: you do not receive hundreds of similar frames of the same moment, but a full visual story of what really happened and how it felt to be there.

Of course there is always some gentle guidance — during couple portraits or group photos with family and friends. We help you find flattering light and relaxed, natural poses that still feel like you. These are the photographs you will print, frame and see at home every day.

Working across Ontario & worldwide

We live in Toronto and happily travel across the GTA, Niagara, Muskoka and all of Ontario. For weddings that are far from the city we often arrive a day earlier, stay overnight near your venue and start the day rested and prepared. This helps us avoid road risks and gives us time to explore the location, study the light and find the best spots for photos.

Our background in destination weddings means that traveling for love stories is second nature to us. We have photographed and filmed celebrations in Montenegro, Croatia (including many weddings in Dubrovnik), Italy, France and other countries. We still regularly fly back to Montenegro and Italy for our couples and partner planners — and we are always happy to travel for new stories.

Whether your wedding takes place in downtown Toronto, on a vineyard in Niagara, in a forest in Muskoka or on a coastline in Europe, our approach stays the same: one cohesive, thoughtful, emotionally rich story of your day.

FAQ

Questions we hear most often

If you are curious about something that is not listed here, just mention it in your inquiry — we are always happy to answer honestly and in detail.

How many photos will we receive?

We edit and deliver all of the good photographs from your wedding day. For a full wedding this is usually around 700–1,000 images, with no artificial limits.

When will we receive our photos and film?

Our usual turnaround time is 3–4 months for full wedding galleries. Films are delivered after the photos and follow a similar timeline depending on the season.

Do you travel outside Toronto and Ontario?

Yes. We regularly work across the GTA, Niagara, Muskoka and all of Ontario, and we still travel abroad for destination weddings in Europe, the US and beyond.

What happens if it rains?

We are fully prepared to keep working in any weather. For engagement and family sessions we can also reschedule if neighbouring dates are free.

How do we book you?

Send us an inquiry with your date and location. We will reply with availability, pricing and next steps, and we can schedule a call, Zoom or in-person meeting to go through all the details. Your date is reserved once the contract is signed and the 30% retainer is paid.

Do you shoot on film?

Yes. We love adding film photography as a beautiful, tactile layer to your story. If you would like film included, let us know in your message.

Do you deliver RAW files?

No. We carefully select and edit all final images and deliver them as high-resolution JPEGs. RAW files remain part of our creative process and are not included.

Do you offer other types of shoots?

Our main focus is weddings, engagements, proposals and family sessions, but we also photograph portraits and brand stories that are built around people and real moments.
